Peach Cake

Ingredients:
 Canned peaches (2)
 Yellow cake mix (1 package)
 Buttter (6 oz)
 Pecans (1/3 cup)
 Nutmeg (1/2 tsp)
 Cinnamon (1 tsp)

 Directions:
 Pour peaches and juice into crockpot. Sprinkle cake mix evenly over peaches. DO NOT MIX. 
 Sprinkle spices and nuts evenly over peaches and cake mix. Cut butter into slices and distribute evenly overtop of other ingredients.

 Cook on HIGH for 2 hours.
